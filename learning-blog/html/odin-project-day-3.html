Lesson 1 - Commit Messages.

This lesson will go over the importance of good commit messages. It will discuss how to write them, when to commit, and why having a history of good commits is so important.


Why Commit Messages are so Important.

  • Employers will look through your projects on GitHub, and therefor your commit history.
  • Having a good commit message history allows you and other developers to quickly see what changers were made and why!
  • Having a good commit message history also helps you if you come back to a project after stepping away from it for a while. As it will help explain the context and thought process present when initially writing out the code.
